## 9.2. Abstrak Asli (Original Abstract)

> Traditional neural networks employ fixed weights during inference, limiting their ability to adapt to changing input conditions, unlike biological neurons that adjust signal strength dynamically based on stimuli. This discrepancy between artificial and biological neurons constrains neural network flexibility and adaptability. To bridge this gap, we propose a novel framework for adaptive neural networks, where neuron weights are modeled as functions of the input signal, allowing the network to adjust dynamically in real-time. Importantly, we achieve this within the same traditional architecture of an Artificial Neural Network, maintaining structural familiarity while introducing dynamic adaptability. In our research, we apply Chebyshev polynomials as one of the many possible decomposition methods to achieve this adaptive weighting mechanism, with polynomial coefficients learned during training. Of the 145 datasets tested, our adaptive Chebyshev neural network demonstrated a marked improvement over an equivalent MLP in approximately 83% of the cases, performing strictly better on 121 datasets. In the remaining 24 datasets, the performance of our algorithm matched that of the MLP, highlighting its ability to generalize the behavior of standard neural networks while offering enhanced adaptability. As a generalized form of MLP, this model seamlessly retains MLP performance where needed while extending its capabilities to achieve superior accuracy across a wide range of complex tasks. These results underscore the potential of adaptive neurons to enhance generalization, flexibility, and robustness in neural networks, particularly in applications with dynamic or non-linear data dependencies.

## 9.3. 10 Referensi Vital Jurnal

Berikut adalah 10 referensi utama yang dirujuk dalam jurnal yang memenuhi kriteria ilmu komputer (menjadi baseline, fondasi teori, penyedia dataset, atau karya terdahulu yang ditingkatkan):

1.  **Bliss, T.V., & Collingridge, G.L. (1993)**
    *   *Citation:* "A synaptic model of memory: long-term potentiation in the hippocampus." *Nature*, 361(6407), 31–39.
    *   *Peran:* Memberikan landasan biologis mengenai plastisitas sinaptik dan memori jangka panjang pada otak manusia yang menginspirasi pengembangan neuron adaptif.
2.  **Bolukbasi, T., Wang, J., Dekel, O., Saligrama, V. (2017)**
    *   *Citation:* "Adaptive neural networks for efficient inference." *International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML)*, PMLR, 527–536.
    *   *Peran:* Menjadi rujukan utama untuk kelas komputasi adaptif makro (pemilihan sub-jaringan) yang kelemahannya diatasi oleh jurnal ini.
3.  **Boyd, J.P. (2001)**
    *   *Citation:* *Chebyshev and Fourier spectral methods*. Courier Corporation.
    *   *Peran:* Menyediakan landasan teoretis untuk analisis stabilitas numerik dan perilaku divergen Polinomial Chebyshev di luar interval $[-1, 1]$.
4.  **Chen, Y., Dai, X., Liu, M., Chen, D., Yuan, L., Liu, Z. (2020)**
    *   *Citation:* "Dynamic convolution: Attention over convolution kernels." *Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR)*, 11030–11039.
    *   *Peran:* Merepresentasikan kelas parameterisasi dinamis (kernel gabungan) sebagai baseline pembanding teoretis.
5.  **Dai, J., Qi, H., Xiong, Y., Li, Y., Zhang, G., Hu, H., Wei, Y. (2017)**
    *   *Citation:* "Deformable convolutional networks." *Proceedings of the IEEE International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV)*, 764–773.
    *   *Peran:* Menyediakan referensi untuk konsep parameterisasi spasial kondisional pada lapisan konvolusi.
6.  **Ha, D., Dai, A., Le, Q.V. (2016)**
    *   *Citation:* "Hypernetworks." *arXiv preprint arXiv:1609.09106*.
    *   *Peran:* Menjadi baseline pembanding utama untuk model pembangkit bobot berbasis jaringan eksternal (*Hyper FCNN*).
7.  **Liu, Z., Wang, Y., Vaidya, S., Ruehle, F., Halverson, J., Soljačić, M., Hou, T.Y., Tegmark, M. (2024)**
    *   *Citation:* "KAN: Kolmogorov-arnold networks." *arXiv preprint arXiv:2404.19756*.
    *   *Peran:* Menyediakan konteks modern mengenai representasi bobot sebagai fungsi univariat (B-splines) pada tepi jaringan saraf.
8.  **Mason, J.C., & Handscomb, D.C. (2002)**
    *   *Citation:* *Chebyshev Polynomials*. Chapman and Hall/CRC.
    *   *Peran:* Sumber referensi utama untuk semua sifat matematika, hubungan rekursif, pembuktian ortogonalitas, dan roots dari Polinomial Chebyshev jenis pertama.
9.  **Olson, R.S., La Cava, W., Orzechowski, P., Urbanowicz, R.J., Moore, J.H. (2017)**
    *   *Citation:* "PMLB: a large benchmark suite for machine learning evaluation and comparison." *BioData Mining*, 10(36), 1–13.
    *   *Peran:* Penyedia 145 dataset klasifikasi benchmark yang digunakan dalam seluruh eksperimen komparatif pada jurnal.
10. **Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., Uszkoreit, J., Jones, L., Gomez, A.N., Kaiser, Ł., Polosukhin, I. (2017)**
    *   *Citation:* "Attention is all you need." *Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS)*, 30.
    *   *Peran:* Rujukan utama untuk mekanisme atensi mandiri (*self-attention*) yang menjadi basis model pembanding *Attention FCNN*.
