## Abstrak Asli (Verbatim)

> Traditional neural networks employ fixed weights during inference, limiting their ability to adapt to changing input conditions, unlike biological neurons that adjust signal strength dynamically based on stimuli. This discrepancy between artificial and biological neurons constrains neural network flexibility and adaptability. To bridge this gap, we propose a novel framework for adaptive neural networks, where neuron weights are modeled as functions of the input signal, allowing the network to adjust dynamically in real-time. Importantly, we achieve this within the same traditional architecture of an Artificial Neural Network, maintaining structural familiarity while introducing dynamic adaptability. In our research, we apply Chebyshev polynomials as one of the many possible decomposition methods to achieve this adaptive weighting mechanism, with polynomial coefficients learned during training. Of the 145 datasets tested, our adaptive Chebyshev neural network demonstrated a marked improvement over an equivalent MLP in approximately 83% of the cases, performing strictly better on 121 datasets. In the remaining 24 datasets, the performance of our algorithm matched that of the MLP, highlighting its ability to generalize the behavior of standard neural networks while offering enhanced adaptability. As a generalized form of MLP, this model seamlessly retains MLP performance where needed while extending its capabilities to achieve superior accuracy across a wide range of complex tasks. These results underscore the potential of adaptive neurons to enhance generalization, flexibility, and robustness in neural networks, particularly in applications with dynamic or non-linear data dependencies.

## 10 Referensi Vital

### 1. Mason & Handscomb (2002) — *Chebyshev Polynomials*
**Penulis:** J.C. Mason, D.C. Handscomb | **Penerbit:** Chapman and Hall/CRC
**Peran:** Referensi utama untuk semua properti polinomial Chebyshev (definisi rekursif, ortogonalitas, extremal properties, akar) yang menjadi fondasi matematika seluruh framework adaptive weighting. Dikutip **paling sering** di seluruh jurnal.

### 2. Koch (2004) — *Biophysics of Computation*
**Penulis:** C. Koch | **Penerbit:** Oxford University Press
**Peran:** Sumber utama untuk penjelasan biologis neuron — threshold behavior, action potentials — yang memberikan inspirasi dan justifikasi biologis bagi pendekatan adaptive neuron.

### 3. Bliss & Collingridge (1993) — *A Synaptic Model of Memory*
**Penulis:** T.V. Bliss, G.L. Collingridge | **Venue:** Nature, 361(6407), 31–39
**Peran:** Referensi kunci untuk konsep [[Long-Term Potentiation]] dan [[Synaptic Plasticity]] — mekanisme biologis inti yang menginspirasi ide adaptive weighting di mana kekuatan koneksi berubah berdasarkan aktivitas.

### 4. Olson et al. (2017) — *PMLB Benchmark Suite*
**Penulis:** R.S. Olson, W. La Cava, P. Orzechowski, R.J. Urbanowicz, J.H. Moore | **Venue:** BioData Mining, 10(36)
**Peran:** Sumber dari **145 dataset klasifikasi** yang digunakan sebagai testbed utama evaluasi empiris. PMLB menyediakan benchmark yang standar dan terkurasi.

### 5. Liu et al. (2024) — *KAN: Kolmogorov-Arnold Networks*
**Penulis:** Z. Liu, Y. Wang, S. Vaidya, F. Ruehle, J. Halverson, M. Soljačić, T.Y. Hou, M. Tegmark | **Venue:** arXiv:2404.19756
**Peran:** Motivasi arsitektural utama — ide merepresentasikan bobot sebagai fungsi (bukan skalar) berasal dari KAN, meskipun implementasinya berbeda (spline vs Chebyshev).

### 6. Troumbis et al. (2020) — *Chebyshev Polynomial Feedforward Neural Network*
**Penulis:** I.A. Troumbis, G.E. Tsekouras, J. Tsimikas, C. Kalloniatis, D. Haralambopoulos | **Venue:** Environmental Modelling & Software, 126, 104663
**Peran:** Karya paling terkait erat — menggunakan Chebyshev dalam neural network tetapi untuk mengaproksimasi output (bukan bobot), dan menggunakan Differential Evolution (bukan backpropagation). Perbedaan-perbedaan ini dibahas secara detail.

### 7. Vaswani et al. (2017) — *Attention Is All You Need*
**Penulis:** A. Vaswani, N. Shazeer, N. Parmar et al. | **Venue:** NeurIPS 2017
**Peran:** Baseline perbandingan (Attention FCNN) dan contoh mekanisme dynamic weighting via attention scores. Digunakan sebagai salah satu arsitektur input-dependent untuk perbandingan kompetitif.

### 8. Ha et al. (2016) — *Hypernetworks*
**Penulis:** D. Ha, A. Dai, Q.V. Le | **Venue:** arXiv:1609.09106
**Peran:** Baseline perbandingan (Hypernetwork FCNN). Mewakili pendekatan di mana satu jaringan menghasilkan bobot untuk jaringan lain — paradigma alternatif untuk dynamic parameterization.

### 9. Trefethen (2019) — *Approximation Theory and Practice*
**Penulis:** L.N. Trefethen | **Penerbit:** SIAM (Extended Edition)
**Peran:** Referensi pendukung untuk properti Chebyshev — terutama kekuatan aproksimasi dan extremal properties. Memperkuat justifikasi matematika untuk pemilihan Chebyshev di atas basis lain.

### 10. Bolukbasi et al. (2017) — *Adaptive Neural Networks for Efficient Inference*
**Penulis:** T. Bolukbasi, J. Wang, O. Dekel, V. Saligrama | **Venue:** ICML 2017, PMLR, pp. 527–536
**Peran:** Contoh utama dari pendekatan *adaptive computation* (early-exit, subnetwork selection) yang mengadaptasi jalur komputasi tetapi bukan bobot — digunakan untuk mempertegas perbedaan filosofis dengan pendekatan Chebyshev yang mengadaptasi bobot itu sendiri.
