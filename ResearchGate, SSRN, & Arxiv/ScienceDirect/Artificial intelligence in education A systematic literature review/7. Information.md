## Abstrak (Asli)

> Artificial intelligence (AI) in education (AIED) has evolved into a substantial body of literature with diverse perspectives. In this review paper, we seek insights into three critical questions: (1) What are the primary categories of AI applications explored in the education field? (2) What are the predominant research topics and their key findings? (3) What is the status of major research design elements, including guiding theories, methodologies, and research contexts? A bibliometric analysis of 2,223 research articles followed by a content analysis of selected 125 papers reveals a comprehensive conceptual structure of the existing literature. The extant AIED research spans a wide spectrum of applications, encompassing those for adaptive learning and personalized tutoring, intelligent assessment and management, profiling and prediction, and emerging products. Research topics delve into both the technical design of education systems and the examination of the adoption, impacts, and challenges associated with AIED. Furthermore, this review highlights the diverse range of theories applied in the AIED literature, the multidisciplinary nature of publication venues, and underexplored research areas. In sum, this research offers valuable insights for interested scholars to comprehend the current state of AIED research and identify future research opportunities in this dynamic field.

## 10 Referensi Paling Vital

1. **Nwana, H. S. (1990). Intelligent tutoring systems: An overview. *Artificial Intelligence Review*.**
    - **Konteks:** Merupakan artikel paling berpengaruh yang menyebabkan lonjakan kutipan tertinggi dalam sejarah AIED, tetap relevan sebagai dasar sistem tutorial cerdas selama puluhan tahun.
2. **Chassignol, M., dkk. (2018). Artificial intelligence trends in education: A narrative overview. *Procedia Computer Science*.**
    - **Konteks:** Menjadi dasar kerangka klasifikasi aplikasi AIED (konten, metode pengajaran, penilaian, dan komunikasi) yang banyak dirujuk dalam jurnal ini.
3. **Goksel, N., & Bozkurt, A. (2019). Artificial intelligence in education: Current insights and future perspectives.**
    - **Konteks:** Menjadi acuan utama bagi metodologi pencarian literatur yang diterapkan oleh penulis dalam jurnal ini.
4. **Aria, M., & Cuccurullo, C. (2017). Bibliometrix: An R-tool for comprehensive science mapping analysis. *Journal of Informetrics*.**
    - **Konteks:** Merupakan pengembang alat analisis "bibliometrix" yang digunakan secara sentral untuk memproses 2.223 artikel dalam penelitian ini.
5. **Felder, R. M. (1988). Learning and teaching styles in engineering education. *Journal of Engineering Education*.**
    - **Konteks:** Salah satu makalah yang paling banyak dikutip dan menjadi landasan bagi banyak sistem AI adaptif yang berfokus pada gaya belajar.
6. **Turing, A. M. (1950). Computing machinery and intelligence. *Mind*.**
    - **Konteks:** Karya klasik yang menjadi fondasi teoretis bagi seluruh bidang kecerdasan buatan, termasuk aplikasinya di dunia pendidikan.
7. **Davis, F. D. (1989). Perceived usefulness, perceived ease of use, and user acceptance of information technology. *MIS Quarterly*.**
    - **Konteks:** Menjadi dasar teori bagi penelitian tentang adopsi dan perilaku pengguna (Technology Acceptance Model) terhadap sistem AI.
8. **Zawacki-Richter, O., dkk. (2019). Systematic review of research on artificial intelligence applications in higher education. *International Journal of Educational Technology in Higher Education*.**
    - **Konteks:** Tinjauan sistematis yang sangat berpengaruh dan menjadi poin perbandingan utama untuk mengevaluasi status AIED di pendidikan tinggi.
9. **Piaget, J. (1964). Cognitive development in children: Development and learning. *Journal of Research in Science Teaching*.**
    - **Konteks:** Merupakan dasar dari Teori Belajar Konstruktivis, yang teridentifikasi sebagai teori yang paling banyak diterapkan dalam makalah empiris AIED.
10. **Chiu, T. K. F., dkk. (2023). Systematic literature review on opportunities, challenges, and future research recommendations of artificial intelligence in education.**
    - **Konteks:** Memberikan pemahaman terbaru tentang dampak AIED terhadap keterampilan abad ke-21 dan tantangan etika yang dihadapi saat ini.
