## Verbatim Abstract (Original English)

> Content-based research paper recommendation (CbRPR) has seen advances in domains like computer science and biomedicine, but remains unexplored for mathematics, a field where paper relatedness is more conceptual than explicit textual or citation-based similarity. In mathematics, papers may be connected through shared proof techniques, logical implications, or natural generalizations, yet exhibit minimal textual or citation overlap, rendering standard embedding or citation-based CbRPR ineffective. To address this gap, we first conduct an expert-driven study characterizing mathematical recommendations, revealing that relevance is inherently aspect-driven. Grounded in this insight, we introduce GoldRiM (small and expert-annotated) and SilverRiM (large and automatically derived), the first datasets for aspect-aware CbRPR in mathematics. Recognizing that LLM embeddings of mathematical content alone yields suboptimal representation, we propose AchGNN, an aspect-conditioned heterogeneous GNN that jointly models textual semantics, citation structure, and author identity. Across GoldRiM and SilverRiM, AchGNN consistently outperforms prior aspect-based CbRPR methods, achieving substantial gains across all evaluated aspects. We conduct ablation studies to analyze the contribution of individual aspect supervisions, authorship lineage, and graph structural signals to AchGNN’s performance. To assess domain generality, we further evaluate AchGNN on the Papers with Code dataset of machine learning publications, demonstrating that our aspect-aware approach effectively transfers beyond mathematics.

## 10 Referensi Vital & Perannya

Pondasi teoretis dan metodologi jurnal ini ditopang oleh sepuluh referensi vital berikut:

1. **Yauhen Babakhin dkk. (2025)**
   * *Judul:* Llama-Embed-Nemotron-8B: A Universal Text Embedding Model for Multilingual and Cross-Lingual Tasks.
   * *Peran:* Menyediakan model embedding [[Memtron]] yang digunakan sebagai baseline pencarian kemiripan teks terkuat untuk sub-tugas matematika Math Stack Exchange.
2. **Joeran Beel dkk. (2016)**
   * *Judul:* Paper recommender systems: a literature survey.
   * *Peran:* Menyediakan survey menyeluruh mengenai sejarah sistem rekomendasi karya ilmiah berbasis isi (CbRPR) yang menjadi landasan latar belakang.
3. **Iz Beltagy dkk. (2019)**
   * *Judul:* SciBERT: Pretrained Language Model for Scientific Text.
   * *Peran:* Menyediakan model bahasa [[SciBERT]] berbasis BERT yang digunakan sebagai model dasar tekstual ilmiah dan dibandingkan dalam evaluasi.
4. **Arman Cohan dkk. (2020)**
   * *Judul:* SPECTER: Document-level Representation Learning using Citation-informed Transformers.
   * *Peran:* Menyediakan model [[SPECTER]] yang melatih representasi dokumen ilmiah menggunakan sinyal sitasi, digunakan sebagai baseline embedding.
5. **William L. Hamilton dkk. (2017)**
   * *Judul:* Inductive representation learning on large graphs.
   * *Peran:* Mengusulkan arsitektur [[GraphSAGE]] yang menjadi dasar perumusan perambatan pesan (*message passing*) heterogen pada AchGNN.
6. **Malte Ostendorff dkk. (2022)**
   * *Judul:* Specialized Document Embeddings for Aspect-based Similarity of Research Papers.
   * *Peran:* Mengusulkan arsitektur [[SciBERT-FT]] Siamese network yang menjadi baseline terbaik sebelumnya di dataset Papers with Code (PwC).
7. **Steffen Rendle dkk. (2009)**
   * *Judul:* BPR: Bayesian personalized ranking from implicit feedback.
   * *Peran:* Merumuskan fungsi loss optimasi peringkat [[BPR Loss]] yang diadaptasi oleh AchGNN untuk memisahkan sampel positif dan negatif.
8. **Ankit Satpute dkk. (2024)**
   * *Judul:* Can LLMs Master Math? Investigating Large Language Models on Math Stack Exchange.
   * *Peran:* Menganalisis kelemahan LLM dalam memahami teks matematika murni yang memotivasi diperlukannya integrasi silsilah kepengarangan dan graf.
9. **Yuning You dkk. (2020)**
   * *Judul:* Graph contrastive learning with augmentations.
   * *Peran:* Mengusulkan metode pembelajaran kontrastif graf [[GraphCL]] yang dijadikan baseline pembanding utama berbasis graf heterogen.
10. **Chenyan Zhang dkk. (2023)**
   * *Judul:* Multi-Aspect enhanced Graph Neural Networks for recommendation.
   * *Peran:* Merumuskan penggunaan pengondisian aspek (*aspect conditioning*) pada graf neural networks yang diadopsi oleh AchGNN dari domain *e-commerce*.
