## Abstrak Asli (Verbatim)

> Content-based research paper recommendation (CbRPR) has seen advances in domains like computer science and biomedicine, but remains unexplored for mathematics, a field where paper relatedness is more conceptual than explicit textual or citation-based similarity. In mathematics, papers may be connected through shared proof techniques, logical implications, or natural generalizations, yet exhibit minimal textual or citation overlap, rendering standard embedding or citation-based CbRPR ineffective. To address this gap, we first conduct an expert-driven study characterizing mathematical recommendations, revealing that relevance is inherently aspect-driven. Grounded in this insight, we introduce GoldRiM (small and expert-annotated) and SilverRiM (large and automatically derived), the first datasets for aspect-aware CbRPR in mathematics. Recognizing that LLM embeddings of mathematical content alone yields suboptimal representation, we propose AchGNN, an aspect-conditioned heterogeneous GNN that jointly models textual semantics, citation structure, and author identity. Across GoldRiM and SilverRiM, AchGNN consistently outperforms prior aspect-based CbRPR methods, achieving substantial gains across all evaluated aspects. We conduct ablation studies to analyze the contribution of individual aspect supervisions, authorship lineage, and graph structural signals to AchGNN’s performance. To assess domain generality, we further evaluate AchGNN on the Papers with Code dataset of machine learning publications, demonstrating that our aspect-aware approach effectively transfers beyond mathematics.

## 10 Referensi Vital

Berikut adalah 10 referensi utama yang menjadi fondasi teoretis, baseline, atau dataset pembanding dalam penelitian ini:

1.  **Babakhin et al. (2025)**
    *   *Judul:* Llama-Embed-Nemotron-8B: A Universal Text Embedding Model for Multilingual and Cross-Lingual Tasks.
    *   *Venue:* arXiv (arXiv:2511.07025).
    *   *Peran:* Dirujuk sebagai Memtron, salah satu model bahasa besar (LLM) dengan representasi embedding terkuat di MTEB yang dievaluasi sebagai baseline.
2.  **Beltagy et al. (2019)**
    *   *Judul:* SciBERT: Pretrained Language Model for Scientific Text.
    *   *Venue:* EMNLP.
    *   *Peran:* Model bahasa berbasis BERT yang dilatih khusus pada korpus teks ilmiah, digunakan sebagai base model untuk inisialisasi dokumen ilmiah.
3.  **Cohan et al. (2020)**
    *   *Judul:* SPECTER: Document-level Representation Learning using Citation-informed Transformers.
    *   *Venue:* ACL.
    *   *Peran:* Baseline utama untuk model embedding tingkat dokumen berbasis sitasi yang sering digunakan dalam perbandingan performa sistem rekomendasi ilmiah.
4.  **Hamilton et al. (2017)**
    *   *Judul:* Inductive representation learning on large graphs.
    *   *Venue:* NIPS.
    *   *Peran:* Memperkenalkan kerangka kerja GraphSAGE yang menjadi basis algoritmik pengiriman pesan heterogen (*heterogeneous message passing*) pada model AchGNN.
5.  **Kardas et al. (2020)**
    *   *Judul:* AxCell: Automatic Extraction of Results from Machine Learning Papers.
    *   *Venue:* EMNLP.
    *   *Peran:* Menyediakan basis dataset Papers with Code (PwC) yang digunakan penulis untuk memverifikasi generalisasi model AchGNN pada domain non-matematika.
6.  **Kreutz and Schenkel (2022)**
    *   *Judul:* Scientific paper recommender systems: a literature review of recent publications.
    *   *Venue:* International Journal on Digital Libraries.
    *   *Peran:* Menyediakan tinjauan pustaka komprehensif mengenai baseline dan perkembangan mutakhir sistem rekomendasi ilmiah.
7.  **Ostendorff et al. (2022)**
    *   *Judul:* Specialized Document Embeddings for Aspect-based Similarity of Research Papers.
    *   *Venue:* JCDL.
    *   *Peran:* Memperkenalkan model SciBERT-FT, baseline sistem rekomendasi paper berbasis aspek terkuat sebelum penelitian ini.
8.  **Rendle et al. (2009)**
    *   *Judul:* BPR: Bayesian personalized ranking from implicit feedback.
    *   *Venue:* UAI.
    *   *Peran:* Memperkenalkan Bayesian Personalized Ranking (BPR) loss yang diadopsi sebagai fungsi loss pemeringkatan utama dalam mengoptimalkan AchGNN.
9.  **Wang et al. (2022)**
    *   *Judul:* Leveraging the fine-grained user preferences with graph neural networks for recommendation.
    *   *Venue:* World Wide Web.
    *   *Peran:* Memperkenalkan arsitektur FigGNN yang menginspirasi implementasi pengkondisian aspek pada tingkat kalkulasi kemiripan graf heterogen.
10. **You et al. (2020)**
    *   *Judul:* Graph contrastive learning with augmentations.
    *   *Venue:* NeurIPS.
    *   *Peran:* Memperkenalkan GraphCL, baseline terkuat berbasis graf kontrasif agnostik-aspek yang dibandingkan langsung dengan AchGNN.
