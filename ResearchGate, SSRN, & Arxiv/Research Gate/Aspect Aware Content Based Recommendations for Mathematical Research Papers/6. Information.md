## Abstrak Asli (Verbatim)

> Content-based research paper recommendation (CbRPR) has seen advances in domains like computer science and biomedicine, but remains unexplored for mathematics, a field where paper relatedness is more conceptual than explicit textual or citation-based similarity. In mathematics, papers may be connected through shared proof techniques, logical implications, or natural generalizations, yet exhibit minimal textual or citation overlap, rendering standard embedding or citation-based CbRPR ineffective. To address this gap, we first conduct an expert-driven study characterizing mathematical recommendations, revealing that relevance is inherently aspect-driven. Grounded in this insight, we introduce GoldRiM (small and expert-annotated) and SilverRiM (large and automatically derived), the first datasets for aspect-aware CbRPR in mathematics. Recognizing that LLM embeddings of mathematical content alone yields suboptimal representation, we propose AchGNN, an aspect-conditioned heterogeneous GNN that jointly models textual semantics, citation structure, and author identity. Across GoldRiM and SilverRiM, AchGNN consistently outperforms prior aspect-based CbRPR methods, achieving substantial gains across all evaluated aspects. We conduct ablation studies to analyze the contribution of individual aspect supervisions, authorship lineage, and graph structural signals to AchGNN's performance. To assess domain generality, we further evaluate AchGNN on the Papers with Code dataset of machine learning publications, demonstrating that our aspect-aware approach effectively transfers beyond mathematics.

## 10 Referensi Vital

### 1. Beel et al. (2016) — Survei Sistem Rekomendasi Makalah
**Referensi:** Joeran Beel, Bela Gipp, Stefan Langer, and Corinna Breitinger. "Paper recommender systems: a literature survey." *International Journal on Digital Libraries* 17, 4 (2016), 305–338.
**Venue:** IJDL
**Peran:** Survei komprehensif yang menjadi landasan literatur CbRPR; dikutip berulang kali sebagai referensi utama untuk menetapkan *state of the art* dan mengidentifikasi gap di domain matematika.

### 2. Cohan et al. (2020) — SPECTER
**Referensi:** Arman Cohan, Sergey Feldman, Iz Beltagy, Doug Downey, and Daniel Weld. "SPECTER: Document-level Representation Learning using Citation-informed Transformers." *ACL 2020*, 2270–2282.
**Venue:** ACL 2020
**Peran:** Salah satu baseline utama; model representasi dokumen berbasis sitasi yang dibandingkan secara langsung dalam evaluasi.

### 3. Beltagy et al. (2019) — SciBERT
**Referensi:** Iz Beltagy, Kyle Lo, and Arman Cohan. "SciBERT: Pretrained Language Model for Scientific Text." *EMNLP 2019*.
**Venue:** EMNLP 2019
**Peran:** Baseline embedding utama; varian fine-tuned (SciBERT-FT) merupakan metode terbaik pada dataset PwC sebelum AchGNN.

### 4. Ostendorff et al. (2022) — SciBERT-FT / Specialized Embeddings
**Referensi:** Malte Ostendorff, Till Blume, Terry Ruas, Bela Gipp, and Georg Rehm. "Specialized Document Embeddings for Aspect-based Similarity of Research Papers." *2022 ACM/IEEE JCDL*.
**Venue:** ACM/IEEE JCDL 2022
**Peran:** Menyajikan SciBERT-FT — baseline terkuat pada PwC dan standar perbandingan langsung; hasil PwC di Table 3 diambil dari paper ini.

### 5. You et al. (2020) — GraphCL
**Referensi:** Yuning You, Tianlong Chen, Yongduo Sui, Ting Chen, Zhangyang Wang, and Yang Shen. "Graph contrastive learning with augmentations." *NeurIPS 2020*, 33, 5812–5823.
**Venue:** NeurIPS 2020
**Peran:** Baseline graf terkuat; GraphCL merupakan pembanding langsung AchGNN yang menunjukkan pentingnya *aspect conditioning* di atas contrastive learning.

### 6. Hamilton et al. (2017) — GraphSAGE
**Referensi:** William L. Hamilton, Rex Ying, and Jure Leskovec. "Inductive representation learning on large graphs." *NIPS 2017*, 1025–1035.
**Venue:** NIPS 2017
**Peran:** Fondasi arsitektural AchGNN; mekanisme message passing heterogen AchGNN terinspirasi langsung oleh GraphSAGE.

### 7. Rendle et al. (2009) — BPR Loss
**Referensi:** Steffen Rendle, Christoph Freudenthaler, Zeno Gantner, and Lars Schmidt-Thieme. "BPR: Bayesian personalized ranking from implicit feedback." *UAI 2009*, 452–461.
**Venue:** UAI 2009
**Peran:** Fungsi loss utama ($\mathcal{L}_{\text{rank}}$) yang digunakan AchGNN untuk mengoptimasi peringkat *aspect-conditioned*.

### 8. Zhang et al. (2023) — MA-GNN
**Referensi:** Chenyan Zhang, Shan Xue, Jing Li, Jia Wu, Bo Du, Donghua Liu, and Jun Chang. "Multi-Aspect enhanced Graph Neural Networks for recommendation." *Neural Networks* 157 (2023), 90–102.
**Venue:** Neural Networks
**Peran:** Inspirasi utama untuk *aspect-conditioned scoring* di AchGNN; model dari domain e-commerce yang diadaptasi ke CbRPR.

### 9. Zhang et al. (2025) — Qwen3 Embedding
**Referensi:** Yanzhao Zhang et al. "Qwen3 Embedding: Advancing Text Embedding and Reranking Through Foundation Models." *arXiv:2506.05176* (2025).
**Venue:** arXiv
**Peran:** Model embedding yang digunakan untuk inisialisasi node dalam AchGNN dan sebagai baseline LLM embedding terkuat pada dataset matematika.

### 10. Kardas et al. (2020) — Papers with Code (PwC)
**Referensi:** Marcin Kardas et al. "AxCell: Automatic Extraction of Results from Machine Learning Papers." *EMNLP 2020*, 8580–8594.
**Venue:** EMNLP 2020
**Peran:** Dataset benchmark lintas-domain untuk evaluasi AchGNN di luar matematika; benchmark standar untuk CbRPR berbasis aspect.
